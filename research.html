Research Interest

I want to work for the sustainable development of my country, Bangladesh. Nearly 200 million people live in Bangladesh which is a massive data source to work with. From hospitals to clinics, doctors and patients are generating valuable information through prescriptions, medical tests, etc which may have a significant impact in healthcare. I want to analyze those data and bring meaningful insight from it blended with ubiqutous computing to associate location intelligence along with disease spreading behaviour detection. I want to implement computer vision algorithms on medical test images like MRI scan, cityscans to develop predictive models that can predict disease from test images.

I have another interest to work with climate data. Through satellite images, its now possible to detect the climate changes happening globally. Platforms like Google earth engine is lubricating the path to obtain such research goals. Detecting changes in environmental data will alert geographical specialists or decision makers to take initiatives ahead of time.
